### 1. 개인정보의 수집 및 이용 (직접 수집 없음)

## English

**Sudoku 250** ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how data is handled in compliance with global privacy standards.

### 1. Information We Do Not Collect (No Direct Collection)
The App does not require any sign-up or login, and does not directly collect or transmit personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number. Your game progress (completed levels, best times, in-progress boards, and settings) is stored only locally on your device and is never sent to our servers.

### 2. Advertising & Third-Party Data Collection (Google AdMob)
The App is provided for free and serves advertisements through Google AdMob. In the process of serving ads, Google and its advertising partners may automatically collect and use certain data to provide relevant ads and measure performance:

- Advertising identifiers (IDFA / Ad ID)
- Device information (device model, OS version, language settings, approximate location, etc.)
- Ad interaction data (clicks, impressions, etc.)

**Your Rights & Choices:**

- **iOS App Tracking Transparency (ATT):** You can choose whether to allow tracking when you first launch the App. Declining will not restrict your access to any features of the App.
- **Device Settings:** You can opt out or reset your advertising identifier at any time by navigating to **Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking** on your iOS device.

For more details on how Google processes your data, please visit:

- Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

### 3. Global User Rights (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)
Users residing in the European Economic Area (EEA), the UK, or California have specific rights regarding their data under laws like GDPR and CCPA. Since we do not collect your data directly, you can exercise your rights (such as opting out of personalized advertising) through the Google Consent Management Platform (CMP) shown in the app or via your device's operating system settings.

### 4. Children's Privacy
The App is not specifically directed to children under the age of 13 (or the legal age in your jurisdiction) and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take immediate steps to delete it.

### 5. Data Retention & Deletion
- **Data collected by Developer:** None. Local game data is permanently deleted when you uninstall the App from your device.
- **Data collected by Third-Parties (Google):** Retained and deleted in accordance with Google's own privacy policy.

This policy may be updated to reflect legal or service changes; updates will be posted in this document.
