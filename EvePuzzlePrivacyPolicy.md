### 2. 수집하는 정보
**(a) 이용자가 직접 제공하는 정보.** 필수 제공 항목이 없습니다. 본 앱에는 자체 회원가입·로그인이 없습니다.

**(b) 기기에만 저장되는 정보.** 게임 진행 상황, 최소 이동 기록(베스트), 사운드/햅틱 설정, 광고 노출 빈도 카운터는 이용자 기기에 로컬로(iOS `UserDefaults`) 저장됩니다. 이 데이터는 기기에 남으며 개발자에게 전송되지 않습니다.

**(c) 광고 데이터(Google AdMob 경유).** 본 앱은 Google AdMob을 통해 광고를 표시합니다. 광고 제공·측정을 위해 Google 및 그 파트너는 다음을 수집·처리할 수 있습니다.
- 기기 및 광고 식별자(허용 시 IDFA, IDFV 등)
- IP 주소 및 이를 통한 대략적 위치
- 기기 정보(모델, OS 버전, 언어)
- 광고 상호작용 및 성과 데이터

iOS에서 IDFA 접근은 Apple의 앱 추적 투명성(ATT) 동의가 필요합니다. 거부하더라도 광고는 계속 표시되지만 IDFA를 이용한 맞춤화는 적용되지 않습니다.

**(d) Game Center(선택).** Apple Game Center에 로그인하여 리더보드를 사용하는 경우, 순위 표시를 위해 Game Center 플레이어 식별자와 점수가 Apple에 의해 처리됩니다. 선택 사항이며, 로그인하지 않으면 리더보드는 생략되고 베스트 점수는 기기에만 남습니다.

당사는 민감정보(특별 범주 개인정보)를 고의로 수집하지 않습니다.

### 3. 정보의 이용 목적

- 리더보드 표시(Game Center 사용 시에 한함)
- 핵심 게임 기능(진행 상황·설정 저장) 동작
- 법적 의무 준수(예: 동의 기록 보관)

### 4. 광고, 동의, 추적
**Google AdMob.** 광고는 Google이 제공합니다. Google의 데이터 처리는 [Google 개인정보처리방침](https://policies.google.com/privacy) 및 [Google 서비스를 사용하는 사이트·앱의 정보 이용 방식](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites)을 따릅니다.

**EEA(유럽경제지역)·영국·스위스(GDPR).** 본 앱은 광고를 로드하기 전에, 필요한 지역에서 Google 인증 동의 메시지(Google User Messaging Platform, "UMP")를 표시하여 이용자가 광고 목적의 데이터 이용을 수락하거나 관리할 수 있도록 합니다. 동의 선택은 이용자 기기에 저장됩니다. 앱 내 **설정 → 광고 동의 관리**에서 동의를 언제든 변경·철회할 수 있습니다.

**Apple 앱 추적 투명성(ATT).** iOS에서 본 앱은 IDFA를 이용한 교차 앱·웹사이트 추적 전에 이용자 동의를 요청합니다.

### 5. 제3자 서비스
- **Google AdMob / Google User Messaging Platform** — 광고 및 동의 관리. Google 개인정보처리방침 참조.
- **Apple Game Center** — 선택적 리더보드. [Apple 개인정보처리방침](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/) 참조.

## English

Eve Puzzle ("the App", "we", "us") is a free, ad-supported puzzle game for iOS developed by Dawoon Choi ("the Developer"). This Privacy Policy explains what information is collected when you use the App, how it is used, and the choices you have. By using the App, you agree to this Policy.

### 1. Who we are
- **App:** Eve Puzzle (iOS)
- **Developer:** Dawoon Choi
- **Contact:** downeve@gmail.com

The App does not require you to create an account, and we do not ask for your name, email address, phone number, or postal address.

### 2. Information we collect
**(a) Information you provide.** None is required. The App has no sign-up or login of its own.

**(b) Stored on your device only.** Game progress, best (fewest-move) records, sound/haptic preferences, and ad-frequency counters are stored locally on your device (iOS `UserDefaults`). This data stays on your device and is not sent to the Developer.

**(c) Advertising data (via Google AdMob).** The App shows ads through Google AdMob. To serve and measure ads, Google and its partners may collect and process:
- Device and advertising identifiers (e.g., IDFA where permitted, IDFV)
- IP address and approximate location derived from it
- Device information (model, OS version, language)
- Ad interaction and performance data

On iOS, access to the IDFA requires your permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt. If you decline, ads are still shown but are not personalized using the IDFA.

**(d) Game Center (optional).** If you are signed in to Apple Game Center and use leaderboards, your Game Center player identifier and scores are processed by Apple to display rankings. This is optional; if you are not signed in, leaderboards are skipped and your best scores remain on your device.

We do not knowingly collect special-category (sensitive) personal data.

### 3. How we use information
- To display and measure advertising (our only source of revenue)
- To show leaderboards (only if you use Game Center)
- To run core game features (saving progress and settings) on your device
- To comply with legal obligations (such as keeping consent records)

### 4. Advertising, consent, and tracking
**Google AdMob.** Ads are provided by Google. Google's handling of data is governed by the [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) and [How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites).

**EEA, UK, and Switzerland (GDPR).** Before loading ads, the App presents a Google-certified consent message (Google User Messaging Platform, "UMP") where required, so you can accept or manage how your data is used for advertising. Your consent choices are stored on your device. You can change or withdraw consent at any time via **Settings → Manage Ad Consent** in the App.

**Apple App Tracking Transparency (ATT).** On iOS, the App requests your permission before any cross-app or cross-website tracking that uses the IDFA.

### 5. Third-party services
- **Google AdMob / Google User Messaging Platform** — advertising and consent management. See the Google Privacy Policy.
- **Apple Game Center** — optional leaderboards. See the [Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

These third parties act as independent controllers/processors of the data they collect through their SDKs.

### 6. Data sharing
The Developer does not sell your personal data and does not share it with third parties for their own marketing. The data described above is processed by Google and Apple under their own policies to provide advertising and Game Center features.

### 7. Data retention and deletion
- Data stored on your device is removed when you delete the App.
- You can reset your advertising identifier in **iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking / Apple Advertising**.
- Where a consent form is shown (EEA/UK), you can change or withdraw your consent via **Settings → Manage Ad Consent** in the App.
- For data held by Google or Apple, refer to their respective policies and privacy controls.

### 8. Children's privacy
The App is not directed to children under 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your country), and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided personal data, please contact us and we will assist.

### 9. Your rights and choices
Depending on your location (e.g., GDPR in the EEA/UK, CCPA/CPRA in California), you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal data, and to withdraw consent.
- **Withdraw/change ad consent (EEA/UK):** in the App via Settings → Manage Ad Consent.
- **Tracking (iOS):** change at any time in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking.
- **Other requests:** contact us at downeve@gmail.com.

### 10. International transfers
Google and Apple may process data in countries other than your own. They implement safeguards (such as Standard Contractual Clauses) as described in their policies.

### 11. Security
We rely on the security measures of Apple iOS and the Google and Apple services we use. On-device data is protected by the iOS app sandbox. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

### 12. Changes to this Policy
We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy. However, for material changes affecting the processing of personal data (such as introducing payments or server-side data collection), we will provide separate in-app notice and obtain fresh explicit consent (an affirmative opt-in under the GDPR). Consent for the processing of advertising data is obtained only through the UMP consent form as an explicit opt-in, never by continued use alone.

### 13. Contact
Questions or requests: **downeve@gmail.com** (Dawoon Choi).

### 2. Información que recopilamos
**(a) Información que proporcionas.** No se requiere ninguna. La App no tiene registro ni inicio de sesión propios.
